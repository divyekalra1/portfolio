Hey! I am Divye Kalra, a first-year MSSI student from Johns Hopkins University, Homewood Campus. Broadly, my research interests include cryptography, network and distributed systems security.
In the past, I have worked at the IITB Trust Lab, Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, as a visiting researcher under the guidance of Prof. Manoj Prabhakaran on a project involving the implementation of a COA-secure cryptographic primitive called CASE. I have also worked in areas like secure multi-party computation with Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs), record linkage, and the design and implementation of stream ciphers and machine-learning based prediction algorithms under the guidance of Dr. Dali Kaafar, Dr. Manish Kumar and Dr. Ankur Bhattacharjee.
